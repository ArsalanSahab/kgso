February 15th-17th, 2019

Modules For This Year

fabrica of andreas vesalius

The most exquisite pleasure in the practice of medicine comes from nudging a layman in the direction of terror, then bringing him back to safety again! This February, put your medical knowledge to test, to survive through a series of challenging, yet extremely fun rounds centered around the medical and ethical principles laid down by Andreas Vesalius. Be it rapid fire, simulations or diagnosis you need to be as engrossed as Him to walk away being a rightful scion of science. You better not find this 'humerus' because Andreas got his lon on you. Remember: 'Every second counts!'

Code Red

Out there in the city, there is a criminal hiding in the dark of the night. You have evidence, however time is limited. Prepare for a crime investigation like never before as 'Code Red' offers delegates a chance to unlock their crime-solving potential and unravel this mysterious affair. Delegates will be tested on their forensic skill, Inquisition and analytical thinking.

Radiance

Radiant. Look at the stars at night, watch the galaxies in motion. It's time to reach higher, beyond the dome of the sky until everything dissolves into jewel-studded black. You will be tested in a way you never have before. You are an astronaut lost in space, an aerospace engineer from the far future. Let the countdown begin!.

Mechanical mayhem

Herein lies the heart and soul of every Science Olympiad. From animals to everyday items, if you look to show off your ingenuity and constructive prowess, this is the module for you. Come journey with us through the ages, as we test your abilities in Mechanical Mayhem..

BiFrost BrainMelt

Can you trace Denaery's family tree to before Aegon's Landing? Navigate Hogwarts without the Marauder's Map? Wondered what purpose occupying your brain with this information serves you? Then this is your time to shine. Bifrost Brainmelt will require a rigorous application of your fandom trivia. Remember if you're not having fun preparing for this module, you're doing it wrong.

Turing's End Game

‘Somebody out there will be able to overcome any encryption technique you use!’ No cipher exists which cannot be decrypted. Nothing is beyond the bounds of possibility; it is you who make it possible. The encryption genie is finally out of the bottle. Do you have what it takes to decrypt the most mind-boggling codes and riddles provided to us by him? Do you think you are the best and decoding is a piece of cake? There are others out there who think they’re one step ahead than you. This module will test or rather challenge your unprecedented cryptography skills. You will have to push your mind to its limits through solving demanding puzzles, ciphers and riddles spread across. This module would not only flex your cerebrum but will also turn out to be a wonderful experience for all the participants. PS. The genie told me these ciphers are alone enough to leave the greatest mind perplexed and overwhelmed..

Cybertage

Ever wanted to test your problem solving skills? Do you think you have what it takes to master this art? Then lets embark this journey where we take you through different modes of programming, and assess your computational knowledge. So are you ready to put your computational skills at test? Ladies and Gentlemen presenting to you Cybertage!.

RoboSapiens

So you really think you have the skills to make a robot? The real question is whether you have the skills to make a robot good enough to replace humans. Why not try out for RoboSapiens at KGSO '19 and show us what you've got

Identity

“The enchanting charms of this sublime science, mathematics, reveal only to those who have the courage to go deep into it” - Carl Friedrich Gauss. This year’s mathematics module will be a platform of intense competition, yet our goal will be to collectively marvel at the aesthetic of math. Expect to see sines of madness as you differentiate between true and false. So muster your passion and prepare to behold this beauty in its raw fullest.

Trials of Apollo

"Logic is the beginning of wisdom." Apollo invites you and present you with the opportunity to sharpen your skills. Join us for a quest through various logic-based problems and challenges that put your intelligence to the test.

Psych-ed

During such a time of awareness and mindfulness, the field of psychology has started to grow like never before. This module at KGSO shows you a little glimpse of what practice in the field may look like. Even if the extent of your psychological knowledge is to listen to and help out your friends, you will experience what it is like to do this professionally! This module will allow you to see, diagnose and treat a patient and then get a chance to carry out researches yourself. The best part? This module requires no prior knowledge of psychology from its participants.
